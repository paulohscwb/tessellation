  <h2 align="center"><img src="vr/salas/imagens/icone.png" style="margin-bottom:-10px" width="45"> Tessellation in strips</h2>
The figures or works that are chosen as tesserae cover or pave the plane or a surface. The result is called tessellation, tiling, paving or mosaic. Tessellations have been used since ancient times in floors, walls, carpets and other objects.
<br>This work shows the tessellations made on the faces of polyhedra, taking advantage of the rectangular symmetries of the faces of these solids.
<br>On this page, we have some examples of tessellations in the form of strips, inserted into the rectangular faces of stellation to infinity polyhedra.
